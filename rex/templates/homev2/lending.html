Lending

Lending is to invest by your own XVG to get the return on a daily basis from the Management of XVG. The highest return per month is up to 48% of your investment. XVG provides investment options for you to get the full benefit as per your investment ability.

You would like to invest more than $1,000, XVG will guarantee your return per day based on your investment as the additional benefit, excluding your daily return.
